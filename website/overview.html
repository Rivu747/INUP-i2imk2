Overview

About

This workshop is being organized both for familiarization and interaction of the participants with faculty members of IITKGP.
Online applications are invited from interested researchers, students (B.Tech 3rd year onwards, M.Sc., M.Tech., PhD, postdoc) and faculty members from academia and R&D laboratories and people from industries for participation in this workshop.
The technical programme includes:-
Familiarization of:
• Broad area of Nano-fabrication and characterization.
• On-going research activities at IITKGP in Nanoelectronics domain.
• Overview of infrastructure at IITKGP.
• Availing IITKGP research facilities through INUP-i2i

Details

• The workshop will be conducted in virtual mode through IT enabled platforms. The link will be sent to the selected participants.
• During the workshop the participants are expected to present PPT (1-2 slides, template of a poster given on the website) which describes his/her research area of interest and plans of research work under INUP-i2i at IIT KGP. The proposals will be provisionally selected for research support at IIT Kharagpur on the basis of your poster presentation.
• An MCQ based Quiz would be conducted based on the topics which are covered during the 3 day workshop.
• Please submit NOC form( Download ) duly signed from the Head of the Institution/Supervisor.
• Start date of online application: 4th January, 2023.
• Last date of online application: 6th February, 2023.
• How to Register for the workshop.
• Selected participants will be notified through email on 1st February, 2023.
